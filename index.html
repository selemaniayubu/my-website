My story  

Hello, my name is Selemani Ayubu, and I am 22 years old. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and ICT with Education at Mzumbe University in Morogoro, Tanzania. My academic journey has been driven by a passion for numbers, technology, and the transformative power of education.

I have a solid foundation in various mathematical disciplines, including algebra, calculus, statistics, and discrete mathematics. In the realm of ICT, I am proficient in programming languages such as JS, C, C++ and Java, and I have hands-on experience with database management and network systems. My coursework and practical experiences have equipped me with the skills necessary to analyze complex problems and devise effective solutions.

In addition to my technical skills, I have a strong understanding of educational theories and teaching methodologies. My experience as a student teacher has allowed me to apply these theories in real classroom settings, where I have developed lesson plans, conducted assessments, and provided valuable feedback to students.
